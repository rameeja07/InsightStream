The new Captain of world cup

"Leadership is not about being the best, but about bringing out the best in your team."

Shreyas Iyer's leadership journey has been remarkable, culminating in his appointment as the captain of the Indian team for the 2025 ICC Champions Trophy and the World Test Championship (WTC) final. This decision was confirmed by BCCI Secretary Jay Shah, who expressed confidence in Iyer's ability to lead the team to victory in these prestigious tournaments. The Times of India Iyer's rise to captaincy was marked by his consistent performances and leadership in the Indian Premier League (IPL). In the 2025 season, he captained Punjab Kings to the final, showcasing his tactical acumen and composure under pressure. His appointment as captain reflects a strategic shift by the BCCI, aiming to build a team for the future while maintaining a balance of experience and youth. Under his leadership, India is poised to compete strongly in the upcoming international tournaments.
