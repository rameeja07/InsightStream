Allahabad University UG Admission 2025: Registration begins for CUET candidates at alldunivcuet.samarth.edu.in

The University of Allahabad has commenced the registration process for undergraduate admissions for the 2025 academic year. Candidates who have qualified the Common University Entrance Test for Undergraduates (CUET UG 2025) can now apply for various UG courses at the university. Candidates can register on the portal at alldunivcuet.samarth.edu.in. Only candidates who have appeared in CUET UG 2025 are eligible to register for undergraduate admission at Allahabad University. During the profile update, applicants must verify that they meet all academic and category-related criteria and upload all required documents correctly. Incomplete or incorrect entries may lead to disqualification from the admission process.
