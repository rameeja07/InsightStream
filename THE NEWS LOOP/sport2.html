Kabaddi League 2025: Thrilling Matches and Rising Stars

"Puneri Paltan is a popular kabaddi team from Pune, known for their strong performances and passionate fans in the Pro Kabaddi League."

Puneri Paltan is having a strong run in the 2025 Pro Kabaddi League Season 12. Recently, they secured a convincing 45–36 victory over Bengal Warriorz, with their captain Aslam Inamdar delivering a Super Raid, while Aditya Shinde and Vishal Bhardwaj contributed impressive performances. The team also dominated Gujarat Giants with a 41–19 win, highlighted by key contributions from Abinesh Nadarajan and Pankaj Mohite, who surpassed 400 PKL points. Under the new leadership of head coach Ajay Thakur , the former Indian captain, the team aims to bounce back from last season’s disappointing finish. They have strengthened their squad by signing raider Sachin Tanwar for ₹1.058 crore, adding more depth and experience to their attack. With this revamped lineup and strategic guidance , Puneri Paltan is poised to be a strong contender for the PKL title this season, and fans are optimistic about their chances of success.
